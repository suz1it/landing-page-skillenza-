Information

As you are aware, humanity generates huge amounts of data. A total of 2.5 Quintillion bytes per day! If our math's correct, that works out to about 30 terabytes every second give or take! That’s huge! But how do you aim to make sense of all this data? One way is machine learning. And that’s where you come in. It doesn’t matter if you have no experience in this domain. After all, some of the best discoveries are done by noobs! For about 86400 seconds (that's 24 hours for you) of hacking, we invite college students all throughout India to come and hack at Banyon Hacks! Give wings to your deep-rooted ideas and show them off to the world!

FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

Can any one participate?
Answer:

This is strictly for students only. However, if you are an experienced developer, a still want to have fun, you can mentor a student team. But you will not be eligible for any prizes.

What is the venue for the challenge?
Answer:

No. 389, 12 Main, 1st Cross, Indiranagar, Bangalore 560038

Is it compulsory to stay back?
Answer:

If you have to go home for the night, we won't stop you. Just come back the next morning. :)

Do I need to bring my laptop?
Answer:

Yes! Don't forget to bring a charger too.

Can I participate as an individual?
Answer:

This is a team event. No lone crusaders, please.

I don't have any team members. Where can I find one?
Answer:

Not to worry. Just hop on over to our Vhackers group in Facebook and list your requirements. If you are still having trouble, we will help you find a team member on the day of the hack.

What is the team size limit?
Answer:

Team size is limited to 4. Any more, and the team might be a little crowded, don't you think?

Does it cost me anything?
Answer:

No. Nothing. You don't even have to pay for food! We will take care of everything. But transportation is your responsibility.
